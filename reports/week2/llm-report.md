# Report on LLM Usage (Week 2)

During the second week of the project, our team utilized the Gemini LLM as an analytical and formatting assistant to streamline Requirements Engineering and DevOps tasks. The specific use cases are detailed below:

* **Requirements Formalization:** Gemini was used to format our raw User Stories into the required Markdown structure. It helped correctly assign MoSCoW priorities, allocate stable IDs, and draft the Notes/Constraints sections based on our raw ideas and customer feedback.
* **Meeting Documentation & Analysis:** The LLM assisted in processing the raw customer meeting transcript. It extracted key discussion points, highlighted validated and rejected assumptions, and drafted the initial structure for both the `customer-meeting-summary.md` and `analysis.md` reports.
* **Interview Preparation:** Gemini helped reformulate our interview questions regarding the "A1-A2 phrases" feature to ensure they were product-focused and adhered to proper elicitation principles, avoiding biased or hypothetical questioning.

*Note: All AI-generated content was thoroughly reviewed, edited, and validated by the team to ensure accuracy and alignment with the customer's vision and course requirements.*
